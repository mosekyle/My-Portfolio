Market Stall Allocation System

The Market Stall Allocation System is a solo project developed to streamline the process of allocating stalls to market vendors. By automating key tasks like vendor registration, stall availability tracking, and allocation, the system improves efficiency and transparency. The project includes sophisticated allocation algorithms that prioritize fairness and vendor preferences, along with communication tools to notify vendors of allocation results.
